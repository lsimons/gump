Rhino team provides fixes for batik
<p>
With the recent restart of Gump nagging, the community has been making a push to 

improve the <A href="http://lsd.student.utwente.nl/gump/#Project+Summary">health of 

interactions</a>. 
</p>
<p>
<a href="http://xml.apache.org/batik/index.html">XML Batik</a> was observered to 

go down, and they were notified. 
<more/>
Their analysis led them to beleive that <a 

href="http://www.mozilla.org/rhino/">Rhino</a> had
<a 

href="http://nagoya.apache.org/eyebrowse/ReadMsg?listName=general@gump.apache.org&

msgId=1341462">broken backwards compatibility</a>. The <a 

href="http://www.mozilla.org/rhino/">Rhino</a> team agreed, and 
committed a change to 

<a 

href="http://nagoya.apache.org/eyebrowse/ReadMsg?listName=general@gump.apache.org&

msgNo=3934">that restored backwards compatibility</a>.
</p>
<p>
<a href="http://xml.apache.org/batik/index.html">XML Batik</a> began to 
<a href="http://lsd.student.utwente.nl/gump/xml-batik/index.html"
>build properly</a> again, and <b>a jar discontinuity was resolved</b> 
before it caused grief.</b>
</p>
